HEADLINE
--------

Nearly everything from the July 3rd review landed: minigames wired, magic
learnable from Mubaba (6 tricks), daily neighbor quests with cardboard-cutout
rewards, Mubaba's quest chain, underground identities (underWorldBldgs.rtf),
5 of 8 underground buildings standing with real art (as of today), 11 of
29 holidays,
Dreamer's Almanac, multi-slot saves, underground music, sun shadows,
click-to-face.

The gap is no longer systems — it's PURPOSE and FEEDBACK:

1. NO LONG-TERM GOAL. Friendship numbers and quest counters exist, but
   nothing the player works toward that visibly changes the world. The
   pieces are all designed and half-built, just unconnected:
   - The 5 empty underground pads are a ready-made progression ladder.
   - The economy is fully DESIGNED in underWorldBldgs.rtf (Recycle Bin
     sells your stuff for IOUs; Gettin' Place's 4 merchants, Stimmy Tim's
     coffee/donuts, and Black Goddess rhythm prizes all use IOUs) and the
     IOU item already exists — but none of the shops are implemented, so
     treasure still does nothing (old C8, now an implementation task).

2. THE ISLAND IS SILENT. Music only plays underground. stars.ceol is in
   Music/ (island theme in progress?). SFX = 3 ambient animal sounds; no
   feedback for harvest / craft / reel / UI / quests.
