Cozy Island Game — Deep Plan Review (July 3)
=============================================

Sources reviewed: all 16 src/ modules, SCOPE.md, PROJECT.md, SpriteUpdate.txt,
June27review.txt, holiday_status.txt, asset folders.

HEADLINE FINDINGS
-----------------

The game is much further along than SCOPE.md suggests. World gen, multi-map
travel (island <-> underground), 57 NPCs with arrivals/shacks/friendship,
hand-written branching dialogue for the entire roster, inventory/crafting with
readable ingredients, gettin' stick with timed reel, gardening, animals
(birds/crabs/seagulls/turtles/cicadas/butterflies), the hog, versioned saves
with autosave, canvas CSS scaling — all done. Several June 27 review items are
already fixed.

But three finished systems are unreachable by the player:

1. MINIGAMES ARE DEAD CODE. TicTacToe and RhythmGame are fully built in
   games.js, but the "Challenge to Game" button hits a placeholder at
   src/dialogue.js:405 that says "Games coming soon!" — one wiring fix away
   from a whole feature.

2. MAGIC IS UNLEARNABLE. magic.js has 5 working tricks, but learnMagic() is
   never called anywhere. No NPC teaches anything, so the Magic tab stays
   empty forever.

3. THE MUSIC ISN'T IN THE GAME. underWorld.wav is composed, but audio.js is a
   no-op stub and assets/audio/ is empty.

Also: Cosmos has full written dialogue but is missing from NPC_DEFS and has no
sprite — he can never spawn.


WHAT CHARLES DOES (creative)

C4. Assign magic teachers.
    Decide which 5 NPCs teach the 5 tricks (Vanish, Movable Feature, Summon
    Fairy, Time Warp, Season Shift), at what friendship level, and write the
    short teaching dialogue for each. Currently teachers are placeholder
    indexes 0-4.

C5. More music + an SFX wishlist.
    underWorld.wav covers the underground. The island needs at least one
    overworld loop (seasonal variants later). Also list the ~8 sounds wanted
    first (harvest, craft, reel-pull, door, dialogue blip, holiday fanfare...).

C6. Cosmos.
    He has dialogue but no character. Write his CharacterDraft entry and draw
    his sprite, or cut him.

C7. New-scale sprites (ongoing, after C1).
    Already started (TreeFull, treeTop variants, Boulder, dock, seasonal

C8. Treasure purpose.
    Treasure items currently do nothing after you pull them. Decide the
    fantasy: sell them? Display shelf in the shack? Museum in the underground?
