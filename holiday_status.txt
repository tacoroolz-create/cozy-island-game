# Seasonal Holiday Mapping (proposed)

The current 6-day holiday cycle means holiday #N lands on day 1 + (N-1) * 6.
Season starts are days 1, 41, 81, and 121. The goal is to place seasonal-anchor
holidays at or very near those starts, and to place animal-themed holidays in
seasons where those animals are most present.

## Sweet Season (days 1-40)
Anchored by spring equinox themes.
- Day 1: **Sweet Valley** — first-harvest offering at the beach altar.
- Day 7: **Garden Day** — planting/gardening kicks off the growing season.
- Day 13: **The Returning Bird** — spring migration, bird appears.
- Day 19: **Compliment a Crab Day** — crabs are active on warming beaches.
- Day 25: **Tourist Time!** — visitors arrive as the island opens up.
- Day 31: **Backflip Day** — high-energy silly celebration of longer days.
- Day 37: **The Petal Path Maker** — flowers in bloom, connect meeting spots.

## Saucy Season (days 41-80)
Anchored by summer solstice / heat themes.
- Day 43: **Peak Saucy** — sunset bonfire, longest day celebration.
- Day 49: **Talk Like an Eagle Day** — birds active, loud sky energy.
- Day 55: **Ab Appreciation Day** — Yogatron visits, summer fitness silly.
- Day 61: **Toast Toss Tournament** — beach tournament, dry toast flies well.
- Day 67: **Snake Run Day** — snakes active in warm grass.
- Day 73: **Hoggy's Birthday** — mid-summer hog celebration.
- Day 79: **Memory Lantern Night** — warm evenings, shore memories.

## Cool Season (days 81-120)
Anchored by autumn equinox / cooling themes.
- Day 85: **Cool Valley** — moon-lit memory walk, lanterns.
- Day 91: **The Flealess Market** — harvest market.
- Day 97: **Lost Mail Day** — breezy season, letters blow around.
- Day 103: **Turtle Crossing Guard Day** — turtles still active before cooling.
- Day 109: **The Picnic Reset** — last comfortable outdoor gathering.
- Day 115: **Name the Island Day** — reflective community vote.
- Day 121: **Well-Wishing Garden** — plant well-wishes before Yeesh.

## Yeesh Season (days 121-160)
Anchored by winter solstice / longest night themes.
- Day 121: **Peak Yeesh** — Everburn bonfire, Papa Yeesh visits at midnight.
- Day 127: **Day of the Island God** — giant turtle, doubled animal spawns.
- Day 133: **Castle of Sticks Day** — stick-gathering for cold-day projects.
- Day 139: **Dig a Hole Day** — digging down, literal underworld expansion.
- Day 145: **Clean Your Room Day** — indoor cozy season.
- Day 151: **Backwards Hats Day** — silly indoor-friendly holiday.
- Day 157: **Familiar Seller** — traveling seller visits during slow season.
