CHARACTER 36: Birch (Talking Tree - Patient Legend-Keeper)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

TURN 1 - Birch:
*A tall, silver-barked tree stands serenely, luminous bark markings glowing softly in the dappled light. His wide, gnarled trunk suggests centuries of quiet observation.*
"Ah. *voice is deep, patient* You approach. *bark markings pulse gently* I am Birch. *leaves rustle* I have been... watching. For a long time. *pause* Would you care to hear a story? Or perhaps... you have one to share?"

PLAYER CHOICES:
[1] "I'd love to hear one of your stories, Birch!"
[2] "How long have you been watching?"
[3] "Do you have a favorite story you tell?"

---

TURN 2A (if player chose 1 - hear a story):
Birch: *branches sway thoughtfully*
"A story? *bark glows warmer* Very well. *leaves whisper* This one is about... the First Rain. *pause* Before the rain, the island was... thirsty. The flowers drooped. The streams were silent. *another pause* Then, one day, the sky decided to... cry. Not from sadness. From... release."

PLAYER CHOICES:
[1] "The sky cried from release? That's beautiful!"
[2] "What happened after the First Rain?"
[3] "Do all trees remember the First Rain?"

---

TURN 2B (if player chose 2 - how long):
Birch: *considers this*
"How long? *bark markings shimmer* Time is... fluid. For trees. *pause* I have seen... seasons come and go. Generations of flowers. The arrival of... many beings. *leaves rustle* You are new. But you are... welcome."

PLAYER CHOICES:
[1] "Thank you for welcoming me, Birch!"
[2] "What's the biggest change you've seen?"
[3] "Do you remember when the dream began?"

---

TURN 3A (if player chose 3 - favorite story):
Birch: *branches extend slightly*
"Favorite? *bark pulses* That is... like asking a parent to choose a child. *pause* But there is one. About... the day Sprig found his walking stick. *leaves glow softly* It was a small day. But important. *pause* Small days often are."

PLAYER CHOICES:
[1] "Sprig's walking stick came from you, didn't it?"
[2] "Why was that day so important?"
[3] "What makes a day 'important' to a tree?"

---

TURN 4A (final - Birch's wisdom):
Birch: *settles into stillness*
"You have... listened. *voice warms* Like the earth listens to rain. *bark markings glow* Return when you need... perspective. Stories grow here. Like leaves. *pause* And like leaves... they fall when ready. To be heard."

*Birch returns to his centuries-long observation, leaves whispering secrets to the wind.*
