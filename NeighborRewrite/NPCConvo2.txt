PLAYER CHOICES:
[1] "Definitely. You're the pinkest flower on the island."
[2] "Maybe they just like your energy."
[3] "I think they like anyone who stands still long enough."

Piko: "The pinkest flower? Oh, that's the nicest thing anyone's ever said without beeping. I hope nobody picks me, though. I'd look very silly in a vase."

PLAYER CHOICES:
[1] "I'd put you on the best windowsill in the shack."
[2] "You're too special to pick."
[3] "Maybe you'd look cute in a tiny pot."

---

TURN 2B (if player chose 2):
Piko: "Energy? Do I have energy? I feel mostly sparkles and warmth. Is that the same thing? I hope it's not too much. I don't want to overwhelm the butterflies."

PLAYER CHOICES:
[1] "It's the perfect amount of energy."
[2] "The butterflies can handle it."
[3] "A little overwhelm never hurt anyone."

---

TURN 2C (if player chose 3):
Piko: "Standing still is easy when the weather is warm. Everything slows down. Even my fans spin slower. It's very peaceful, like being a little pink statue of friendship."

PLAYER CHOICES:
[1] "Friendship statue is a good job."
[2] "You'd be the cutest statue in a garden."
[3] "I should try standing still more often."

---

TURN 3AA (if player chose 1 then 1):
Piko: "The best windowsill? Then I'd get morning light and afternoon naps and maybe a view of the beach. That sounds like the best day a robot could have. Thank you, Dreamer."

TURN 3AB (if player chose 1 then 2):
Piko: "Too special to pick. I like that. Maybe I'm not a flower at all. Maybe I'm a whole garden. A tiny, pink, beeping garden."

TURN 3AC (if player chose 1 then 3):
Piko: "A tiny pot? I'd have to fold my arms very carefully. And my antennae would stick out. But I'd try. For you, I'd try anything cute."

TURN 3BA (if player chose 2 then 1):
Piko: "Perfect amount? That's a relief. I don't have a dial for that. I only have a dial for brightness, and it's always on maximum."

TURN 3BB (if player chose 2 then 2):
Piko: "The butterflies can handle it. Good. Because sometimes I beep when I'm happy, and I don't want to startle my tiny wing-flapping friends."

TURN 3BC (if player chose 2 then 3):
Piko: "A little overwhelm never hurt anyone? I hope that's true. I overwhelmed my own charging station once. It blushed and shut down for a whole afternoon."

TURN 3CA (if player chose 3 then 1):
Piko: "Friendship statue is a good job. I'd wear a little plaque at my feet. Plaque words: Piko. Likes butterflies, warm breezes, and Dreamer."

TURN 3CB (if player chose 3 then 2):

TURN 3CC (if player chose 3 then 3):

[3] "Maybe the wind is just spontaneous."

[3] "You should run the whole island."

PLAYER CHOICES:
[1] "Purposeful standing is a real skill."

---

TURN 3AA (if player chose 1 then 1):

TURN 3AB (if player chose 1 then 2):
Rivet: "Order is underrated. Everyone's running around picking weeds and chasing crabs. Someone needs to watch the leaves fall in a sensible pattern."

TURN 3AC (if player chose 1 then 3):
Rivet: "Spontaneous? Spontaneous is just a fancy word for unplanned. I don't trust unplanned. Unplanned things usually end up dented."

TURN 3BA (if player chose 2 then 1):
Rivet: "Serious? Suspicious leaves are always serious. They land too flat, or too curled, or with a smug little edge. You know the type."

TURN 3BC (if player chose 2 then 3):
Rivet: "Run the whole island? Hmph. I'd have a schedule for everything. Crab parades at dawn. Tree inspections at noon. Mandatory leaf alignment at sunset."

TURN 3CA (if player chose 3 then 1):
Rivet: "Purposeful standing is absolutely a skill. It requires balance, patience, and the ability to look busy while doing nothing. Not everyone can manage it."

TURN 3CB (if player chose 3 then 2):
Rivet: "Pretend you don't know? Fine. But if you slip up, I'll deny everything. I have a very convincing 'I was just counting leaves' face."

TURN 3CC (if player chose 3 then 3):
Rivet: "Don't say 'whatever you say' like that. It sounds like you don't believe me. Which is fine, because I don't care if you believe me. Much."═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

[2] "That's a very thorough system."

[3] "Anomalies are more interesting."

PLAYER CHOICES:

[2] "I leave the sky to experts like you."
[3] "Maybe the clouds prefer simplicity."

---

TURN 3BB (if player chose 2 then 2):

TURN 3BC (if player chose 2 then 3):
Cypher: "Anomalies are always interesting. Today's anomaly is a cloud shaped almost exactly like a teapot. I don't drink tea, but I respect the reference."

TURN 3CA (if player chose 3 then 1):

TURN 3CB (if player chose 3 then 2):
Cypher: "Experts? I wouldn't call myself an expert. I'm more of an enthusiastic amateur with an unusually detailed spreadsheet. But thank you for the promotion."

TURN 3CC (if player chose 3 then 3):

CHARACTER 5: Om-Bit (Robot - Monk)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

TURN 1 - Om-Bit:
"Dreamer. Welcome. I have been sitting. The trees do not hurry, and so I try not to hurry either. Would you like to sit with the quiet for a moment?"

PLAYER CHOICES:
[1] "I'd like that very much."
[2] "I'm not very good at quiet."
[3] "Only if you tell me what the trees are thinking."

---

TURN 2A (if player chose 1):
Om-Bit: "Then we sit. Not for a reason. Not to solve anything. Just to be here, where the grass is warm and the sky is wide. This is enough."

PLAYER CHOICES:
[1] "It really is enough."
[2] "I usually need a reason to stop moving."
[3] "Sitting with you feels peaceful already."

---

TURN 2B (if player chose 2):
Om-Bit: "Not good at quiet. That is also a kind of practice. The mind chatters like a small bird. We do not silence the bird. We let it fly past."

PLAYER CHOICES:
[1] "That's a gentle way to think about it."
[2] "My mind chatters a lot."
[3] "What if the bird wants to stay?"

---

TURN 2C (if player chose 3):
Om-Bit: "The trees are not thinking in words. They are being. Tall, patient, rooted. We could learn from trees, though we should not try to grow bark."

PLAYER CHOICES:
[1] "No bark-growing. Got it."
[2] "Patience is the hardest part."
[3] "Being tall seems nice, though."

---

TURN 3AA (if player chose 1 then 1):
Om-Bit: "Enough. Yes. Enough is a small word, but it holds a great deal. Today, we have enough sun, enough breeze, enough presence."

TURN 3AB (if player chose 1 then 2):
Om-Bit: "Needing a reason to stop is a habit of the busy world. Here, we may stop simply because we have arrived. Arrival is reason enough."

TURN 3AC (if player chose 1 then 3):
Om-Bit: "Peaceful. Good. Peace is not a place we reach. It is a place we return to, again and again, like a favorite path through the grass."

TURN 3BA (if player chose 2 then 1):
Om-Bit: "Gentle is good. The world is loud enough. We do not need to be louder than it. We can be the soft place where noise settles."

TURN 3BB (if player chose 2 then 2):
Om-Bit: "A chattering mind is not a problem. It is only the wind moving through empty rooms. Let it move. The rooms remain still."

TURN 3BC (if player chose 2 then 3):
Om-Bit: "If the bird wants to stay, we offer it a branch. We do not chase it away, nor do we make it the master of the house. It stays until it is ready to fly."

TURN 3CA (if player chose 3 then 1):
Om-Bit: "No bark-growing. Wise. Some lessons are not meant to be taken literally. The trees would be confused, and you would be itchy."

TURN 3CB (if player chose 3 then 2):
Om-Bit: "Patience is difficult because the world rewards speed. But trees do not speed. They grow in silence, and no one applauds, yet they become tall."

TURN 3CC (if player chose 3 then 3):
Om-Bit: "Being tall has its own troubles. More wind, more weather, more responsibility to shade the small things. Height is a kind of service."═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
