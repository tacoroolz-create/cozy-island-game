CHARACTER 25: Fluffernox (Monster - Kawaii)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

TURN 1 - Fluffernox:
"Dreamer! Yeesh season is here and everything got fluffy and cold. I tried to catch a snowflake on my paw and it melted, but I am still very proud of the attempt."

PLAYER CHOICES:
[1] "Proud attempts count as catches."
[2] "Snowflakes are tricky."
[3] "Cold and fluffy is a nice combination."

---

TURN 2A (if player chose 1):
Fluffernox: "They do? Then I have caught hundreds of snowflakes in my heart. My heart is very full and slightly damp."

PLAYER CHOICES:
[1] "A damp heart is a busy heart."
[2] "Hundreds is an impressive count."
[3] "Heart catches last forever."

---

TURN 2B (if player chose 2):
Fluffernox: "Tricky and shy. They float down all pretty, then disappear as soon as you love them. Maybe that is their magic."

PLAYER CHOICES:
[1] "Shy magic is still magic."
[2] "Pretty things are allowed to hide."
[3] "Magic that disappears is still real."

---

TURN 2C (if player chose 3):
Fluffernox: "It is! My fuzz gets extra fuzzy and the cold makes my cheeks pink. I look like a warm cloud that somebody chilled."

PLAYER CHOICES:
[1] "Warm clouds are the best clouds."
[2] "Pink cheeks are cozy."
[3] "Extra fuzzy is extra huggable."

---

TURN 3AA (if player chose 1 then 1):
Fluffernox: "Busy and happy! A damp heart means feelings are moving around. Mine are doing little snow dances."

---

TURN 3AB (if player chose 1 then 2):
Fluffernox: "Impressive and probably true. I lost count after twelve but I kept feeling proud, so I rounded up to hundreds."

---

TURN 3AC (if player chose 1 then 3):
Fluffernox: "Forever! The snowflake is gone but the proud feeling stayed. That's basically the same as keeping it in a jar."

---

TURN 3BA (if player chose 2 then 1):
Fluffernox: "Still magic! Quiet magic, quick magic, magic that only your eyes get to see for a second. I like polite magic."

---

TURN 3BB (if player chose 2 then 2):
Fluffernox: "Allowed and smart. If everything stayed visible, the world would be too crowded. Snowflakes know about personal space."

---

TURN 3BC (if player chose 2 then 3):
Fluffernox: "Real because I felt it. The cold little tap on my paw happened. That is enough proof for me."

---

TURN 3CA (if player chose 3 then 1):
Fluffernox: "Best kind of cloud. Warm inside, cold outside, like a fuzzy contradiction wrapped in me."

---

TURN 3CB (if player chose 3 then 2):
Fluffernox: "Cozy like a surprise. My cheeks are saying thank you to the weather even though my toes disagree a little."

---

TURN 3CC (if player chose 3 then 3):
Fluffernox: "Huggable and true! I give very soft hugs. In Yeesh season they also come with a free bonus chill."

PLAYER CHOICES:
[1] "Viking rumors are pretty silly."
[2] "You have plenty to complain about."
[3] "Not caring is your brand."

---

TURN 2A (if player chose 1):
Grumble: "Silly is one word. Vikings? Here? Doing what? Borrowing rocks? I am not worried. I am just... prepared to be unimpressed."

PLAYER CHOICES:
[1] "Prepared unimpressed is safe."
[2] "Rocks don't need borrowing."
[3] "Worry looks good on nobody."

---

TURN 2B (if player chose 2):
Grumble: "Plenty? I have a menu of complaints. Cold toes, sad flowers, quiet nights that last forever. I am not ordering them all at once. That would be excessive."

PLAYER CHOICES:
[1] "A menu is organized complaining."
[2] "Don't order everything at once."
[3] "Excessive complaining is still complaining."

---

TURN 2C (if player chose 3):
Grumble: "Brand? I don't have a brand. I have a personality. A strong one. With layers. The top layer is grump and the bottom layer is also grump."

PLAYER CHOICES:
[1] "Consistent layers are honest."
[2] "Grump is a valid aesthetic."
[3] "At least you're warm inside."

---

TURN 3AA (if player chose 1 then 1):
Grumble: "Safe and dignified. If vikings arrive, I will cross my arms and look skeptical. That usually solves things."

---

TURN 3AB (if player chose 1 then 2):
Grumble: "They don't. The island has enough rocks. Any viking looking to borrow one is going to be disappointed and then politely ignored."

---

TURN 3AC (if player chose 1 then 3):
Grumble: "Nobody. Worry wrinkles your face and I am too young and lumpy for that. I will stick to mild suspicion."

---

TURN 3BA (if player chose 2 then 1):
Grumble: "Organized and efficient. Today I complain about the cold. Tomorrow maybe the sad flowers. Viking rumors can wait until after lunch."

---

TURN 3BB (if player chose 2 then 2):
Grumble: "I won't. Pacing is important. A well-paced complaint lands better than a complaint avalanche."

---

TURN 3BC (if player chose 2 then 3):
Grumble: "Still complaining, but with rhythm. There's an art to it. I am basically a complaint composer."

---

TURN 3CA (if player chose 3 then 1):
Grumble: "Honest and layered. What you see is what you get, which is mostly frowning and occasional flower-straightening."

---

TURN 3CB (if player chose 3 then 2):
Grumble: "Valid and low maintenance. I don't need fancy colors. I just need a cold rock to sit on and an opinion."

---

TURN 3CC (if player chose 3 then 3):
Grumble: "Inside where? Don't say my heart. That's a cliché. But yes. Maybe. A small warm spot. Near the flower-straightening muscle."

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

[2] "Sad flowers need warm thoughts."
[3] "Viking rumors are probably just wind."

PLAYER CHOICES:
[1] "Story energy makes sense."
[2] "The sea deeper than the sky is poetic."
[3] "Winter being loud is a good metaphor."

---

[1] "No further drooping is a win."
[2] "Frost elegance is real elegance."
[3] "Flowers appreciate the effort."

PLAYER CHOICES:
[1] "Rumors compress data nicely."
[2] "Ice kings sound less scary."
[3] "Wind is a bad messenger."

---

TURN 3AA (if player chose 1 then 1):
Gizmo: "It does! Flowers are basically tiny libraries. In Yeesh season they switch to a slower reading speed."

---

TURN 3AB (if player chose 1 then 2):
Gizmo: "Poetic and possibly true. The sky has depth too, but the sea keeps its cold where we can touch it. That is generous data sharing."

---

TURN 3AC (if player chose 1 then 3):
Gizmo: "A good metaphor. Winter arrives with announcements. Cold wind, crunching grass, viking-shaped rumors. Loud in a data-rich way."

---

TURN 3BA (if player chose 2 then 1):
Gizmo: "A documented win. I have charts. Well, I have mental charts. The flower stayed approximately the same. Success."

TURN 3BB (if player chose 2 then 2):

---

TURN 3BC (if player chose 2 then 3):
Gizmo: "Effort counts statistically. Even if the flower cannot say thank you, the universe notes the attempt. I am fairly certain."

---

TURN 3CA (if player chose 3 then 1):
Gizmo: "Nicely. They take a big event and shrink it into a bite-sized worry. Efficient, if not accurate."

---

TURN 3CB (if player chose 3 then 2):
Gizmo: "Less scary and more decorative. Ice kings sound like they would build very pretty castles and then apologize for the chill."

---

TURN 3CC (if player chose 3 then 3):
