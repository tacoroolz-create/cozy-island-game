[1] "Leaf piles are nature's soft landing."
[2] "Crisp air does taste like apples."
[3] "A cozy blanket party sounds wonderful."

[2] "Floating through is still landing."
[3] "Nice sounds are a good gift."

[2] "Expired plant matter has dignity."
[3] "Satisfying textures deserve respect."

[2] "Shouting orange is a tree's right."

[1] "Tolerating a wrap is still cozy."
[2] "Cool air is a breathable blanket."
[3] "Mind-blankets are advanced."

---

TURN 3AA (if player chose 1 then 1):
Wisp: "A good sound. The kind of sound that doesn't demand attention but gets it anyway. I will not call it charming. I will call it competent."

---

TURN 3AB (if player chose 1 then 2):
Wisp: "Dignity in decay. The leaves fell with color. That's more than most things manage."

---

TURN 3AC (if player chose 1 then 3):
Wisp: "Respect, yes. Texture is underrated in this dream. Everyone focuses on visuals and ignores the crinkle."

---

TURN 3BA (if player chose 2 then 1):
Wisp: "A quiet mention from me would be earth-shattering. I prefer to let my gauze speak for itself, which it does, softly and well."

---

TURN 3BB (if player chose 2 then 2):
Wisp: "Their right, apparently. The season gave them a stage and they took it. I would do the same if I had leaves."

---

TURN 3BC (if player chose 2 then 3):
Wisp: "Not unseemly, just loud. I suppose if you're going to change color, you might as well commit to it."

---

TURN 3CA (if player chose 3 then 1):
Wisp: "Still cozy, technically. I am wrapped in tolerable air and surrounded by acceptable stars. Do not call it contentment."

---

TURN 3CB (if player chose 3 then 2):
Wisp: "A breathable blanket. That sounds like something a poet would say and then regret. I, however, will stand by it."

---

TURN 3CC (if player chose 3 then 3):
Wisp: "Advanced and somewhat pretentious. But yes, I have achieved a mental blanket. It's grey and faintly judgmental, like me."

TURN 3CA (if player chose 3 then 1):

TURN 3CB (if player chose 3 then 2):

---

TURN 3CC (if player chose 3 then 3):
