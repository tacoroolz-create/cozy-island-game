CHARACTER 9: Boo-Boo (Ghost - Kawaii)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

TURN 1 - Boo-Boo:
"Dreamer, look at the sky! The clouds are all puffy and dramatic, like they're putting on a show just for us. The shadows are getting longer too, and my gauze got brushed by a brisk breeze. It tickled. Some leaves are starting to change at the edges, like they're thinking about being gold. I want to watch stars tonight."

PLAYER CHOICES:
[1] "Dramatic clouds are like sky paintings."
[2] "Brisk breezes are good for tickling gauze."
[3] "Leaves thinking about gold is cute."

---

TURN 2A (if player chose 1):
Boo-Boo: "Sky paintings! Big soft ones that move. I wish I could paint, but I can only float through things and say 'boo.' Clouds are better painters than me."

PLAYER CHOICES:
[1] "Floating through clouds is a kind of art."
[2] "Your 'boo' is better than painting."
[3] "Moving paintings are extra special."

---

TURN 2B (if player chose 2):
Boo-Boo: "Good tickling. The breeze doesn't mean to be rude, it just wants to say hello to my edges. I giggle every time my gauze flutters."

PLAYER CHOICES:
[1] "Edge hellos are polite."
[2] "Gauze fluttering looks pretty."
[3] "Giggling at breezes is sweet."

---

TURN 2C (if player chose 3):
Boo-Boo: "Cute! They're shy about changing color. First a little yellow, then a little orange, then a big 'ta-da!' I like trees that rehearse."

PLAYER CHOICES:
[1] "Rehearsing trees are patient."
[2] "Little colors count too."
[3] "Ta-da is a good season word."

---

TURN 3AA (if player chose 1 then 1):
Boo-Boo: "A kind of art, yes! I float through clouds and they don't mind. They just keep painting around me. I'm part of the sky now."

---

TURN 3AB (if player chose 1 then 2):
Boo-Boo: "Better? Really? A 'boo' is just a tiny sound, but if you say it's art then it's art. Thank you, Dreamer."

---

TURN 3AC (if player chose 1 then 3):
Boo-Boo: "Extra special because nobody gets bored of them. They change while you watch. Like a very slow surprise."

---

TURN 3BA (if player chose 2 then 1):
Boo-Boo: "Polite and soft. The breeze never grabs. It just touches the parts of me that are floatiest and says 'hi there, little ghost.'"

---

TURN 3BB (if player chose 2 then 2):
Boo-Boo: "Pretty? I feel like a tiny kite. A pink, round, giggly kite with a bow. That's a nice thing to feel like."

---

TURN 3BC (if player chose 2 then 3):
Boo-Boo: "Sweet because the wind doesn't know it's being funny. It just wants to play. I always want to play too."

---

TURN 3CA (if player chose 3 then 1):

---

TURN 3CB (if player chose 3 then 2):

---

TURN 3CC (if player chose 3 then 3):
Boo-Boo: "Ta-da! When the trees all turn at once it's like the island shouting 'surprise!' I hope I'm there to clap."
