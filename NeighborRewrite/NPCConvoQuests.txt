NPCConvoQuests.txt - Cozy Island Game Quest Dialogue (NeighborRewrite Roster)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
Variable placeholders:
  {giver}   = name of the NPC offering the quest
  {target}  = name of the neighbor who needs the item or message
  {item}    = small island object: smooth rock, pretty shell, yellow flower,
              shiny pebble, sea glass piece, palm fruit, nice leaf,
              tiny driftwood stick, warm feather, round seed, sea sponge,
              moon shell
  {season}  = current season: Sweet, Saucy, Cool, Yeesh

Quest types used in this file:
  DELIVER  = {giver} asks Dreamer to take an {item} to {target}
  FETCH    = {giver} asks Dreamer to bring them an {item} found on the island
  RETURN   = {giver} asks Dreamer to take a borrowed {item} back to {target}
  MESSAGE  = {giver} asks Dreamer to tell {target} a short seasonal hello

All lines are spoken words only. No asterisks or action descriptions.
