CHARACTER 1: Chester (Robot - Retired Mechanic)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

TURN 1 - Chester:
*Chester's rusted wheels creak as he rolls up, his trucker hat tilted sideways. His clamp-hands gesture wildly.*
"Heya, kid! *laughs wheezily* So I was tellin' this seagull, right? 'Your wing's loose, pal!' And he just flies off! HA! Good one, huh? Anyway, you look like someone who appreciates fine machinery. What brings ya to my neck of the beach?"

PLAYER CHOICES:
[1] "I'd love to hear more about your mechanic days, Chester!"
[2] "Actually, I'm looking for someone who can fix things around here."
[3] "That seagull joke was AMAZING! Tell me another!"

---

TURN 2A (if player chose 1):
Chester: *chuckles, sparks flying from his neck joint*
"Mechanic days? HA! I once fixed a toaster with nothin' but a paperclip and determination! *laughs at own memory* Thing still works today, prob'ly. You remind me of my old apprentice—always askin' questions. That's good! Questions mean you're thinkin'!"

PLAYER CHOICES:
[1] "What's the most unusual thing you've ever fixed?"
[2] "Do you miss working on machines, or is retirement better?"
[3] "Can you teach me some of your repair tricks?"

---

TURN 3A (if player chose 2):
Chester: *clamps click together thoughtfully*
"Fix things, huh? Well, I'm retired, see? But... *leans in conspiratorially* I never said I stopped HELPIN'. What's broken? My rates are reasonable—payment in oil or good jokes. HA! Just kiddin', kid. Free for friends."

PLAYER CHOICES:
[1] "It's actually my fishing rod—keeps getting stuck."
[2] "The island's old clock tower stopped working."
[3] "I was hoping you could look at my bicycle later!"

---

TURN 4A (if player chose 3):
Chester: *laughs so hard he wobbles on his wheels*
"Another one?! Alright, alright... *clears throat mechanically* So a screwdriver walks into a bar, right? And the bartender says, 'We don't serve your kind here!' And the screwdriver says—*wheezing laughter*—'That's fine, I'm just here for the SCREWS!' HA! Get it? SCREWS?!"

PLAYER CHOICES:
[1] *laugh politely* "You're hilarious, Chester!"
[2] "That was terrible... but I love it!"
[3] "I have a joke too! Want to hear it?"

---

TURN 5A (final - any path):
Chester: *settles down, his green eyes glowing warmly*
"You're alright, kid. Most folks don't stick around for my stories. *adjusts trucker hat* Tell ya what—come back anytime. I got more jokes, more stories, and if somethin' breaks... well, my clamps are pretty good in a pinch. HA! See ya around!"

*Chester rolls away, still chuckling to himself.*
