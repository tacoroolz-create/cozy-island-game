# Day of the Island God

## Core
A day honoring the Island God. A giant turtle sprite appears on the east beach. The number of naturally occurring animals doubles for the day.

## Existing Systems Used
- Shore/east beach tiles
- Animal spawn system
- Player interact
- Giant turtle sprite (new asset by user)

## Gameplay Flow
- Morning: the Island God (giant turtle) appears on the east beach.
- All naturally spawning animals (birds, crabs, seagulls, turtles, butterflies, cicadas) have their spawn counts doubled for the day.
- The giant turtle does not move or speak when interacted with.
- On interaction, flavor text appears describing the player's mindblown awe at the glory of the Island God.

## Neighbor Interaction
- Neighbors reference the day with reverence, wonder, or playful confusion.
- Some neighbors may be found near the beach gazing at the giant turtle.

## Reward
- No item reward. The experience is the reward.
- Possible tiny friendship boost for visiting the beach and observing.

## Constraints
- Giant turtle is a static, non-speaking NPC.
- No new animal types; only spawn rates change.
- No new animal AI behavior.

## New Assets Needed
- Giant turtle sprite (user will create)
