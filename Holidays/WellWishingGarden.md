# Well-Wishing Garden

## Core
A visiting gardener arrives with a cart of potted flowers and encourages everyone to plant one near someone else's door as a silent well-wish. The player can place a flower tile near a neighbor's house and get a sweet, slightly confused thank-you line.

## Existing Systems Used
- Flower harvestables / flower tiles
- Neighbor house/door locations
- NPC dialogue
- Player interact / placement

## Gameplay Flow
- Morning: the gardener appears near the dock or town center with a cart of potted flowers.
- Player receives one "well-wishing flower" by interacting with the gardener.
- Player walks to any neighbor's house and places the flower near the door.
- The flower appears as a temporary decorative tile for the day.
- When the player next talks to that neighbor, they respond with a sweet, confused thank-you.

## Neighbor Interaction
Each neighbor has a unique reaction:
- **Zora:** "A flower by my door? Did you... wish me well? Thank you, I think."
- **Gearwick:** "I accept this floral well-wish on behalf of all gears."
- **Nix:** "I don't sleep, so the wish must be for my waking hours. Thank you."

## Reward
- Small friendship boost with the neighbor who receives the flower.
- At sunset, all well-wishing flowers gently wilt and vanish, leaving no clutter.

## Constraints
- Uses existing flower sprites or a potted variant.
- No negative implications — this is pure well-wishing.

## Optional Upgrade
- If the player well-wishes every neighbor in one cycle, the gardener gives them a "perpetual blessing seed" — a rare flower that blooms once per cycle near the player's house. Flavor only.
